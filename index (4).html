Key Takeaway
The aggregate remains above zero with 3 of 5 indicators in positive territory. DC Permit shows strong filing and land-acquisition momentum. Electrical Equipment lead times remain elevated, reflecting advance orders placed well ahead of need given chronic supply constraints. The two indicators worth watching: Queue Reversal and AI Financing — the most forward-looking signals — are both negative. DRAM rebounded sharply in May with spot momentum turning strongly positive — but DC Permit's approval quality continues to deteriorate, with 13 active regulatory blocks and a new cycle-low approval rate. The cycle is still positive; the leading signals suggest a pace that might begin to moderate.
